# QDL Omni Engine

**Turn your skills and interests into income in minutes.**

QDL Omni Engine generates personalized, actionable business systems based on your input:
- Your skill or interest
- Monthly income target
- Available daily time

## Features
- Generate 3 business systems instantly
- Full deployment blueprint per system
- Vault of pre-built, battle-tested income systems
- History tracking of all generated systems
- Dashboard with progress stats

## How to Use
1. Open the app at [Live Demo](https://qdl-omni-engine.vercel.app)
2. Enter your skill/interest, income target, and time
3. Generate systems and pick one to deploy
4. Follow the deploy blueprint to get started quickly
